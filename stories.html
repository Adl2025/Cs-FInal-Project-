Real Stories

Standing Up Against Bullying

Ali noticed a classmate being bullied online. He reported it to a teacher, who took action and stopped the bullying. Ali learned that speaking up can make a difference and that everyone has the right to feel safe at school.

What can you learn from this? If you see bullying, don't ignore it. Reporting helps protect everyone and makes your school safer.

Knowing Your Rights with Police

When stopped by police on the way home, Aigerim remembered her rights. She calmly asked why she was being stopped and called her parents. The officers explained, and she was able to leave safely. Knowing your rights helps you stay safe and confident.

What can you learn from this? Stay calm, know your rights, and don't be afraid to ask for help from adults you trust.
